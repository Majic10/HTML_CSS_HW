How To Cook Moroccan Tajine

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken drumsticks
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 small carrot, chopped
  • 1 small zucchini, chopped
  • 1 preserved lemon, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Spices

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a Tajine or heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the chicken drumsticks and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes. Remove chicken and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, add onion and garlic. Cook until softened, about 3 minutes.
  4. Add the chopped tomato, carrot, zucchini, eggplant, and preserved lemon to the pot and stir.
  5. Mix together the ground cumin, ginger, cinnamon, paprika, coriander, turmeric, salt, and pepper in a small bowl, then add to the pot. Stir until the vegetables are coated with the spices.
  6. Place the chicken drumsticks on top of the vegetables.
  7. Add enough water to cover the chicken and vegetables.
  8. Bring to a simmer and reduce heat to low. Cover the pot and let cook for about 40-45 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  9. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve hot.
